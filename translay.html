TRANSLAY · 2025

Translate any screen. Stay where you are.

An iPhone overlay translator. Double-tap to translate the screen in two seconds, without leaving the app — DeepL when online, on-device Gemma when not.

CHAPTER 01

One tap. Stay where you are.

Open WeChat, Safari, Instagram — anywhere with text. Double-tap the back of your iPhone. Two seconds later the translation is layered exactly over the original, line by line, in place.

The translator never owns your screen — it borrows it for a moment. No app switch, no clipboard, no copy-paste. One shortcut, one overlay, the original layout left untouched.

CHAPTER 02

Online when you want speed. Offline when you can't.

DeepL on a network, Gemma on the device. The same shortcut, two engines underneath — picked by where you are, not what you toggled.

On-device Gemma means a plane cabin, a subway tunnel, a country with no data plan — none of it is a wall. OCR runs locally too, so the screenshot itself never leaves the device.

CHAPTER 03

Built for any screen, any language.

Chinese, Japanese, Korean, English, German, French, Russian, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese — and more. Pick a target language once; every overlay after that respects it.

Gaming a Japanese RPG, shopping on a Russian site, reading a German contract — Translay treats them all the same way: the line of text in front of you, rendered in the language you read.
